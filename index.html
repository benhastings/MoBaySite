Overview of This Work

A labor of love, the idea began after Amy's most recent visit to Jamaica. The christians in Montego Bay have been assembling in a rented garage for more than 20 years. A number of dedicated christians who have visited and worked with the christians in Montego Bay are co-ordinating this work to construct a building where the brethren can meet with some level of peace and safety.

The plannning is complete and work can begin as soon as the funds are raised.
